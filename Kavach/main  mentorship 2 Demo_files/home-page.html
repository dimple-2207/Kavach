College Mentorship Program

What is it ?

The college mentorship program is a four-day one-on-one personalized session that includes a variety of surveys and interviews to determine the mentee's interests, dream college, course preferences, and academic aspirations.

Who is it for ?

It is for all high school students who are perplexed and have yet to determine which course and subsequent college is ideal for them. This program will enable them to network with seniors and graduates from their chosen college and course, as well as develop an academic strategy for board exams.
