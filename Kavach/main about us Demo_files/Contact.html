Know About Us

Kavach is a utilitarian edu-management and career consulting start-up, which provides top-notch personalised academic assistance, career guidance and mentorship to high school students at the most affordable prices. It's a one-stop solution for all academic and career problems of students who struggle in identifying the right opportunities and careers for themselves.
The venture provides top-notch mentorship from educationists, student mentors from top colleges and universities of the country and other professionals and caters to the needs of different high school students, majorly from 9th to 12th grade.
At Kavach, you can get guidance and sessions on career, academics, interview preparation, tedious application processes and mental health, all on one platform.

OUR JOURNEY


The ideation of Kavach began in June 2020 by our two co-founders, Shrest and Saanya and after vigour work under Research and Development, the first event was launched in July 2020. The organisation started taking pace in September 2020 when it was recognised by Start-Up India. In November 2020, we conducted our first bootcamp with a reach of 7000 students and within six months of our initiation, by February 2021, we successfully created an enthusiastic team that had catered to 80+ mentees with the help of 150+ mentors. We started off with a small team of 5 members which has now become an enthusiastic team of 50 contributing members. Over this period of time, we have been recognised by media platforms and handles and have been backed up by different esteemed organisations.
