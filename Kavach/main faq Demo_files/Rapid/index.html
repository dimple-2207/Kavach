FACTS & QUESTIONS

Get top-notch mentorship with Kavach! Have queries? Get answers to the FAQs

  • Who are the mentors who’d guide us if I apply to Indian universities?

    Answer. The mentors who guide mentees are student mentors, toppers, undergraduates from top universities across the country and experienced researchers.

  • How would you provide assistance in selecting the best college and course for me?

    Answer. Our experienced research team conducts a deep analysis and refers to a set format to question and understand your candidature in the initial sessions. These interactions and questionnaires form the basis of the research and analysis which brings us to the final options which would be the best fit for you.

  • How can I apply for your programs?

    Answer. You can apply for our programs by directly filling this form. Once you’ve filled in the same, an executive shall reach out to you to explain the details of the particular program after which you can enrol yourself.

  • Will the mentorship be time-based or session-wise?

    Answer. The mentorship will be session-wise. However, we shall cater to all your needs through consistent mentoring till you’ve been admitted to your preferred college(s).

  • Is it important to score well in board examinations?

    Answer. The degree and extent of importance differ from individual to individual. However, it’s important to maintain a good academic score to open up more options. If you’re considering merit-based admission then board examinations become of utmost importance.

  • What would be the key areas of focus in the college mentorship program?

    Answer.In the college mentorship program, the mentors will focus on fulfilling all such aspects which are essential for getting the mentee admitted into the best college for them. From helping them identify the best course for them to familiarizing the mentees with the requirements of such courses and colleges. Additional help will be provided for acing the boards examination in order to get admitted into the preferred college.

  • Will I get an opportunity to know someone one on one from my dream college?

    Answer.Yes, in the college mentorship program, the mentees will be given a chance to select two dream colleges and their preferred courses in it and Kavach will connect the mentees to students from their dream college for a session.

  • When should we start preparing for foreign admissions?

    Answer.The earlier, the better it is to start preparing for foreign admissions as it allows the students to build a better portfolio. Starting the preparation while being in Class 11th is also advisable as it gives two proper years for the preparation of tests, portfolio building etc. Students who decide to study abroad after boards can start preparing for it just then and take a gap year until the next intake opens.

  • Is there any option of choosing more than one program at a time to avail offers?

    Answer.Yes, Kavach allows mentees to choose as many programs as they want, for example, College Mentorship Program for India and Global Ed-Consulting Program for foreign preparation. Suitable offers and concessions will be provided if multiple programs are chosen by the mentees.

  • Is the SAT required to apply abroad?

    Answer.The requirements for admission tests vary from country to country and college to college. SAT is considered popular in US colleges but admissions to colleges in countries like Canada, UK can be done without the SAT.

  • Why are programs offered by Kavach so affordable?

    Answer.Kavach is a start-up that focuses on the overall development of the youth. The main motto of Kavach is to make career counselling and guidance affordable for everyone so that every student can make an informed and calculated choice which caters to their strengths, areas of interest, etc. which is why the programs are offered at such affordable rates!

  • Who would guide me if I enrol for Global Ed-Consulting Programs?

    Answer.Kavach has a pool of resources and mentors to help students get the best counselling and guidance for foreign admissions. The mentees will be guided by certified counsellors, student mentors and experienced researchers.
